Our Story

Design
Runs in
the Family

A father-son duo with an unusual origin story. Rajiv — a Dubai-based NRI who spent decades immersed in the world of luxury living — decided to come home to India and build the house he had always dreamed of. His son Arjun, an IIT-Delhi and IIM-Ahmedabad graduate, had developed the same quiet obsession: investing in homes, renovating them, and discovering that the craft of building beautiful spaces was a calling, not a hobby.

Together, they have executed homes for themselves — importing ultra-luxurious fittings from Dubai, guiding a team of skilled craftsmen to replicate exact designs pinned on Pinterest, and learning every nuance of what makes a home truly exceptional.

"Beautiful Homes, Built Openly."
